The primitive underneath

Revocation isn't a policy. It's a key rotation.

Everything here stands on NIP-DA — Scoped Data Grants: private, live, revocable data over public relays. You publish an encrypted scope under your own key and hand its key to whoever needs it. They always read your current version. And when you want them out, you don't edit an access list on someone's server — you rotate the key, and their copy simply stops opening. No policy to trust. No account to close. No company in the middle. The signature is the authorization; the rotation is the revocation.

Nvoy

Delegation

Delegate data to an AI agent, end-to-end encrypted to its key, dereferenced live, severable in one keystroke. OAuth hands out a token into a system that keeps your data; Nvoy hands out the data — and lets you un-see it.

Nontact

Emergent views

Your address book isn't a database — it's the sum of what people currently grant you. Update your card once; everyone holding it dereferences the new truth. Move, change numbers, drop someone — one edit, everyone current.

Nvelope

Blob scale & lifecycle

Live document sharing where a shared file is a scope: revocation instead of expiring links, recovery from your key alone. Big payloads ride encrypted on Blossom with the key delivered by grant — same wire, bigger cargo.

Nherit

Succession

A family break-glass legacy vault: one estate record in per-beneficiary scopes, heirs holding revocable grants, the whole thing reconstituting from a single key printed on paper. No company between you and your family — nothing to be breached or sunset in thirty years.

Ntrigue

Secrets as stakes

A phones-only party game of secrets, dilemmas, and blackmail. Every answer is an encrypted scope; pairwise prisoner's-dilemma trades decide who sees what. Its design law is the protocol's honesty clause played for keeps: you can revoke a secret, but you can't un-tell it.

Notegate

Anonymous intake

Serverless secure tip intake for newsrooms: a tip line is a keypair, sources are ephemeral browser-minted keys whose only reply channel is a 12-word phrase, proof-of-work is the spam toll — checked before anything is decrypted. The grant index is the case docket.

Nscope

The foundation · NIP-DA

Scoped Data Grants — the ~300-line primitive every app above vendors identically. Three event kinds: the encrypted scope, the gift-wrapped grant, the self-encrypted index. A NIP submitted to the nostr protocol, with TS and Go implementations in live interop.
